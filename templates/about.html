Mission & Vision

Empowering Indian Agriculture through Computer Vision.

BreedSense AI is a sophisticated, high-precision identification tool designed specifically for farmers, veterinarians, and livestock management agencies. By instantly analyzing phenotypic characteristics, the platform dramatically reduces manual identification errors, empowering professionals to make rapid, data-driven breeding and healthcare decisions.

At its core, the system is powered by an advanced ResNet-50 Deep Learning model. The architecture has been rigorously fine-tuned on a highly curated dataset encompassing five primary indigenous cattle breeds critical to the subcontinent: Gir, Sahiwal, Red Sindhi, Tharparkar, and Kankrej.

Core Technological Capabilities

  • Real-time Inference: Lightning-fast processing pipeline enabling instantaneous field predictions and rapid usability.
  • Grad-CAM Explainability: Visual heatmap generation ensuring highly transparent and interpretable AI classification decisions.
  • Secure Architecture: Integrated with a scalable MongoDB backend for robust, encrypted data logging and audit trails.
