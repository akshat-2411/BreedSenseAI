About BreedSense AI

BreedSense AI is a capstone project that applies deep learning to the identification of Indian cattle breeds. Accurate breed recognition helps farmers, veterinarians, and livestock agencies make better breeding and healthcare decisions.

The system uses a ResNet-50 model fine-tuned on a curated dataset of five indigenous breeds — Gir, Sahiwal, Red Sindhi, Tharparkar, and Kankrej. Predictions are served through a Flask web application with a MongoDB backend for logging.
